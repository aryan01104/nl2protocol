Seed HEK293 cells into a 96-well plate for a drug treatment experiment:

Seed 10,000 cells per well in 100uL complete DMEM medium across the entire 96-well plate.

Setup:
- Reservoir contains cell suspension at 100,000 cells/mL in complete DMEM
- Destination is a sterile 96-well tissue culture plate

Protocol:
1. Mix the cell suspension reservoir gently before starting to ensure even cell distribution
2. Distribute 100uL of cell suspension to all 96 wells of the plate (columns 1-12, rows A-H)
3. Use the same tip for distribution since it's a homogeneous cell suspension
4. Work column by column to maintain consistent cell settling

Tips:
- Keep cell suspension cold if possible to slow settling
- Work quickly to ensure even distribution
- User should gently rock plate after seeding to distribute cells evenly
- Incubate at 37C, 5% CO2 overnight before treatment
