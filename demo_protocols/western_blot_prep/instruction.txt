Prepare protein samples for SDS-PAGE Western blot analysis:

Setup:
- Tube rack A1-A6 contains 6 protein lysate samples (20-50 uL each)
- Tube rack B1 contains 4X Laemmli sample buffer (with BME/DTT already added)
- Tube rack B2 contains protein ladder/marker
- Temperature module for sample denaturation

Protocol:
1. Transfer 15uL of each protein sample from A1-A6 to fresh tubes in C1-C6
2. Add 5uL of 4X Laemmli buffer to each sample tube (C1-C6) for 1X final concentration
3. Mix each sample 5 times at 15uL to ensure complete mixing with loading buffer
4. Add 5uL of protein ladder to tube C7 (ready-to-load, no buffer needed)
5. Set temperature module to 95°C
6. Pause - user transfers sample tubes to temperature module
7. Comment: "Denature samples at 95C for 5 minutes"
8. Pause for 5 minutes - samples denaturing
9. Set temperature module to 4°C to cool samples
10. Pause - samples ready for loading onto gel

Final volumes: 20uL per sample (15uL lysate + 5uL 4X buffer), 5uL ladder
User loads 10-20uL per lane depending on gel capacity.

Use fresh tips for each protein sample to prevent cross-contamination.
