Perform plasmid DNA extraction from 8 bacterial culture samples using a magnetic bead-based miniprep kit:

Setup:
- Deep well plate column 1 (A1-H1) contains 1mL overnight bacterial cultures (already pelleted)
- Reservoir A1 contains Resuspension Buffer (with RNase A)
- Reservoir A2 contains Lysis Buffer
- Reservoir A3 contains Neutralization Buffer
- Reservoir A4 contains Magnetic Bead suspension
- Reservoir A5 contains Wash Buffer 1
- Reservoir A6 contains Wash Buffer 2 (80% ethanol)
- Reservoir A7 contains Elution Buffer (TE or water)
- Magnetic module holds the processing plate

Protocol:
1. Add 250uL Resuspension Buffer to each bacterial pellet (A1-H1 of deep well plate)
2. Mix vigorously 10 times at 200uL to resuspend pellets completely
3. Add 250uL Lysis Buffer to each well
4. Mix gently 5 times at 200uL - do not vortex (avoid shearing genomic DNA)
5. Pause for 3 minutes - allow complete lysis
6. Add 350uL Neutralization Buffer to each well
7. Mix immediately 5 times at 300uL
8. Pause - user centrifuges plate to pellet debris, then transfers 750uL cleared lysate to magnetic plate
9. Add 30uL Magnetic Beads to each well of magnetic plate
10. Mix 10 times at 200uL to bind DNA to beads
11. Pause for 5 minutes - allow DNA binding
12. Engage magnetic module - beads collect at side
13. Pause for 2 minutes - beads fully separated
14. Aspirate and discard supernatant carefully (avoid disturbing beads)
15. Add 500uL Wash Buffer 1, mix 5 times
16. Engage magnet, pause, remove wash
17. Add 500uL Wash Buffer 2, mix 5 times
18. Engage magnet, pause, remove wash
19. Repeat Wash Buffer 2 step
20. Pause for 5 minutes - air dry beads (do not over-dry)
21. Disengage magnet, add 50uL Elution Buffer
22. Mix 10 times to resuspend beads and elute DNA
23. Engage magnet, pause for 2 minutes
24. Transfer 45uL eluted DNA to clean tubes/plate

Use fresh tips for each sample throughout to prevent cross-contamination.
