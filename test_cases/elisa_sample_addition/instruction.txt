Prepare an ELISA plate with 8 patient samples, each in a 7-point 2-fold serial dilution plus blank, run in duplicate:

1. First, add 100uL of sample diluent (reservoir A1) to columns 2-8 of the dilution plate (rows A-H) for the dilution series
2. Transfer 200uL of each patient sample from tube rack positions A1-D1 and A2-D2 to column 1 of the dilution plate (rows A-H)
3. Perform serial dilution: transfer 100uL from column 1 to column 2, mix 5 times at 150uL, then column 2 to 3, and so on through column 7. Use the same tip for each sample's dilution series.
4. Transfer 50uL from each well of the dilution plate (columns 1-8, rows A-H) to the ELISA plate in duplicate - first set goes to columns 1-8, second set goes to columns 9-12 (only columns 1-4 used for second replicate due to plate width)
5. Add 50uL of detection antibody from reservoir A2 to all sample wells in the ELISA plate
6. Pause for user to incubate plate for 1 hour at room temperature

Change tips between different patient samples to prevent cross-contamination.
