Run a magnetic bead cleanup:
1. Set temperature module to 4°C
2. Engage magnetic module for 5 minutes to pellet beads
3. Aspirate 150uL of supernatant from the deep well plate and discard to waste reservoir
4. Add 200uL of 80% ethanol from the ethanol reservoir to the deep well plate
5. Wait 30 seconds, then remove ethanol
6. Repeat ethanol wash once more
7. Air dry beads for 5 minutes with magnets engaged
8. Disengage magnets, add 50uL of elution buffer from tube rack, mix 10 times at 40uL
9. Engage magnets for 2 minutes
10. Transfer 45uL of eluate to a fresh PCR plate
