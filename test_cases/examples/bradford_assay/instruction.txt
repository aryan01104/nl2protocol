Perform a Bradford protein assay with 8 unknown samples and a BSA standard curve:

Setup:
- Tube rack A1-A8 contains 8 unknown protein samples
- Tube rack B1 contains BSA stock solution (2 mg/mL)
- Reservoir A1 contains Bradford reagent (Coomassie Brilliant Blue G-250)

Protocol:
1. Prepare BSA standard curve in column 12 of the 96-well plate:
   - A12: Add 10uL of BSA stock (2000 ug/mL)
   - B12: Add 5uL BSA stock + 5uL water (1000 ug/mL)
   - C12: Add 2.5uL BSA stock + 7.5uL water (500 ug/mL)
   - D12: Add 1.25uL BSA stock + 8.75uL water (250 ug/mL)
   - E12: Add 1uL BSA stock + 9uL water (200 ug/mL)
   - F12: Add 10uL water only (blank, 0 ug/mL)
2. Add 10uL of each unknown sample to wells A1-A8 (use fresh tip for each)
3. Add 200uL of Bradford reagent to all wells containing samples (A1-A8 and A12-F12)
4. Mix each well gently 3 times at 100uL
5. Pause protocol - user incubates 5 minutes at room temperature, then reads absorbance at 595nm
