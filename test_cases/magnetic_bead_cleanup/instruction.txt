Perform magnetic bead DNA cleanup on 24 samples in column 1-3 of the sample plate:

Setup: Reservoir A1 contains magnetic beads, A2 contains 80% ethanol, A3 contains elution buffer. The sample plate with DNA samples should be placed on the magnetic module.

Protocol:
1. Add 50uL of magnetic beads from reservoir A1 to each sample well (A1-H3), mix 10 times at 100uL to resuspend beads thoroughly
2. Pause for 5 minutes to allow DNA binding
3. Engage magnets and pause for 3 minutes to pellet beads
4. Carefully aspirate and discard 140uL of supernatant from each well, aspirating slowly from the side opposite the magnets
5. First ethanol wash: Add 200uL of 80% ethanol from reservoir A2 to each well. Wait 30 seconds, then remove ethanol.
6. Second ethanol wash: Repeat step 5 with fresh ethanol
7. Pause for 5 minutes with magnets engaged to air-dry beads (do not over-dry)
8. Disengage magnets
9. Add 30uL of elution buffer from reservoir A3 to each well, mix 10 times to resuspend beads
10. Engage magnets and pause 2 minutes
11. Transfer 25uL of eluate (containing purified DNA) to the elution plate in corresponding positions, being careful not to disturb the bead pellet

Use fresh tips for adding clean reagents. Reuse tips within the same sample for waste removal steps.
